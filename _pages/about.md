I am a researcher and teacher working primarily on multiphase flow. My work has an interdisciplinary character at the interface of <br>
* engineering
* applied mathematics
* computational science
* physics
and aims to improve the quality of computer simulations of problems in mechanics with emphasis on fluid dynamics.
